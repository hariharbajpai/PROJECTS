    The Author

    Duduzile Noeleen Ngwenya

    Style: Art, Fiction

    Ngwenya is known for her two poetry collections, Things I Never Said To Myself and This Time You Return To Yourself, which are intended to help people heal and build meaningful relationships with themselves. Things I Never Said To Myself is a self-love collection that aims to help people identify self-sabotage habits, cultivate self-esteem, and learn how to have healthy relationships with themselves and others.
